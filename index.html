About Event

HACKCODEX 1.0 is a student-held coding event organised by Team Pro Club in the month of February. It is an event where participants are challenged to use their coding skills and hack intriguing life problems based on the various themes provided during the event. It aims to encourage the coders to think innovatively by stepping out of the customary realm of textbooks,and stepping into the functional, application based domain. As an initiative taken to provide a plethora of opportunities to all the creative minds working in technical domain, HACKCODEX 1.0 inspires them to articulate solutions of problems in a given time refrain.

We, at Pro Club aim to provide a platform for students to come together and participate actively in tech-savvy sessions and discover the pool of underlying possibiities. It started as an insightful idea of few enthusiastic students of Oriental Group of Institutes, Bhopal. Revolving around the central idea of competitive coding, this time we are bringing HACKCODEX 1.0 .

FAQ

What is the criteria of participation?
Students enrolled in any Indian institution/university are eligible to participate in HACKCODEX. All team members of a team must belong to the same institution.
What should i bring to the venue?
You need to bring your ID-card for identity verification. You have to carry your own laptop.
